Key Regulatory Updates for 2024

Enhanced Nitaqat Requirements

New color classification system with stricter compliance measures for industrial sectors. Companies must maintain minimum Saudi employee ratios.

Effective: January 2024

Overtime Regulation Changes

Revised overtime calculation methods and maximum working hours per week. New penalties for non-compliance in industrial zones.

Effective: March 2024

Workforce Localization

Mandatory Saudization quotas increased for technical and managerial positions. Enhanced training requirements for local employees.

Effective: June 2024

2024 Compliance Checklist

1
Documentation & Records

  • Maintain updated employee contracts
  • Keep attendance and payroll records for 5 years
  • Document training programs and certifications

2
Operational Requirements

  • Implement proper workplace safety protocols
  • Ensure compliance with working hour regulations
  • Maintain proper insurance coverage
